Epidemic Markov model

Epidemic lifetime

The configuration process XX is an irreducible Markov process on a large yet finite state space with an absorbing state (the configuration where no individual is ill). It is therefore guaranteed that the process will eventually converge to this absorbing state. The question that remains is how fast this convergence occurs.

The effective diffusion rate is defined as ρ=βδρ=βδ. The duration and strength of the epidemic depends on this effective diffusion rate but also on the topology of the underlying social network. Intuitively, the more connected the network, the easier for the disease to spread.
